OUR ROOTS

The Aniyam Foundation

"During the November winter of 2019, Aniyam was founded as a collective that would distribute warm clothes and quilts to the squatters in the Madurai city."

The Tamil word Aniyam (அணியம்) comes from the folk performing art Parai Isai and it denotes the performance-ready state of the Parai artistes.

To us, Aniyam stands for the service-ready state of our volunteers. Over time, we gradually expanded our work into various other charity and bold cultural domains to deeply support marginalised sections of society.
